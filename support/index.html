Purchases

Coin packs are optional consumable in-app purchases. If a purchase completes but coins do not appear, restart the app and open the shop so StoreKit can finish any pending transaction.

If the issue continues, email support with the purchase date, product name, and the App Store receipt status shown by Apple. Do not send payment card details.
