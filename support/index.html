Need help with Spell Scramble? Send the details below with your report.

What To Include

  • Device model and iOS version.
  • App version and build number.
  • Language selected in the game.
  • A short description of what happened.

Purchases

Coin packs are optional consumable in-app purchases. If a purchase completes but coins do not appear, restart the app and open the coin shop so StoreKit can finish any pending transaction.
