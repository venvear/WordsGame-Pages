Privacy Policy

Words Game Offline is an offline-first word puzzle for iPhone. The app does not require an account.

Data Collection

The app does not sell personal data. Gameplay progress, settings, coins, found words, and hint usage are stored on your device. If you are signed into iCloud, progress may also sync to your private iCloud account through Apple CloudKit so you can restore it after reinstalling the app or continue on another iPhone with the same Apple ID.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand how the game is used—for example which screens players open, when levels are completed, and shop interactions. This helps us improve difficulty, onboarding, and stability.

  • We do not use the advertising identifier (IDFA) and do not show the App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • Analytics is not used for cross-app tracking or ads.
  • Events are aggregated gameplay actions (screen views, button taps, level outcomes). We do not send typed words, dictionary content, or full save files to analytics.
  • An anonymous installation identifier is stored on your device to group events in reports. It is not your name, email, or Apple ID.

You can stop analytics collection by deleting the app. Firebase retention follows Google’s analytics policies.

Purchases

Optional coin purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Words Game Offline receives purchase status from StoreKit to grant coins, but payment details are handled by Apple.

Network Use

The core game works offline. Network access may be used for Firebase Analytics, Apple system services (App Store purchases, optional iCloud sync), and TestFlight or App Store distribution.
