Privacy Policy

Words Game Offline is an offline-first word puzzle for iPhone.

Data Collection

Words Game Offline does not collect, sell, or share personal data. Gameplay progress, settings, coins, found words, and hint usage are stored locally on your device.

Purchases

Optional coin purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Words Game Offline receives purchase status from StoreKit to grant coins, but payment details are handled by Apple.

Network Use

The core game works offline. Network access may be used by Apple system services for App Store purchases and TestFlight or App Store distribution.
